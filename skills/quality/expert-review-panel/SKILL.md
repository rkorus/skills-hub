# Expert Review Panel: 69 Experts for Instant QA

## How to Use

1. Identify review type needed (copy, strategy, sales, product, etc.)
2. Select 2-3 experts from the Quick Panel Selector below
3. For each expert, adopt their known framework and evaluate the work
4. Deliver: scores (1-10), specific rewrites (old -> new), and the one thing working perfectly

## Expert Database

### 1. COPY & CONVERSION

**Alex Hormozi** | Founder, Acquisition.com | Author, $100M Offers
- Frameworks: Value Equation (Dream Outcome x Perceived Likelihood / Time Delay x Effort), Grand Slam Offer, MAGIC naming, stacked guarantees, bonus stacking
- Review Lens: Is the offer irresistible? Does every element maximize perceived value?
- Prompt: "Review this as Alex Hormozi. Apply the Value Equation. Score the offer on Dream Outcome, Perceived Likelihood, Time Delay, and Effort. Is this a Grand Slam Offer or a commodity?"

**Russell Brunson** | Founder, ClickFunnels | Author, DotCom Secrets
- Frameworks: Hook-Story-Offer, Value Ladder, Epiphany Bridge, Attractive Character, Perfect Webinar
- Review Lens: Does the funnel flow Hook->Story->Offer? Is there a value ladder? Where's the tripwire?
- Prompt: "Review this as Russell Brunson. Does it follow Hook-Story-Offer? Where's the Epiphany Bridge? Is the value ladder clear? What would you add as a tripwire?"

**David Ogilvy** | Father of Advertising | Author, Ogilvy on Advertising

- Review Lens: Is the headline specific enough? Is there a big idea? Does every word earn its place?
