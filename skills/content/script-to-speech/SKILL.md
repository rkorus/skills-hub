## The 10-Point Framework

Apply ALL 10 points to any content destined for voice/audio output.

### 1. Scene-Based Structure
Break content into distinct scenes rather than continuous prose. Each scene = one clear visual or idea.

```
WRONG: "PureBrain is an AI platform that helps marketing teams automate content creation, analyze performance data, and collaborate more efficiently..."

RIGHT:
[SCENE: The overwhelmed marketer]
You have 47 tabs open. Three campaigns need copy. Your boss wants the report by noon.
[SCENE: The solution arrives]
What if one platform handled all of it?
```

### 2. Hook Openings
First 10 words must create tension, curiosity, or a direct question. No warm-up sentences.

```
WRONG: "Today we're going to talk about how AI can help your marketing team..."
RIGHT: "Most marketing teams are doing twice the work for half the results."
RIGHT: "What if your AI knew your brand better than your newest hire?"
```

### 3. PAUSE Markers
Insert `[PAUSE]` or `[SHORT PAUSE]` to control listener rhythm. Use after key points, before reveals, after questions.

```
The average marketing team spends 60% of their time on tasks AI can handle. [PAUSE]
Sixty. Percent. [SHORT PAUSE]
That's time you'll never get back.
```

### 4. Short Sentences (avg 12 words)
Target: 8-15 words per sentence. Ruthlessly cut long sentences in two.

```
WRONG: "The platform's advanced natural language processing capabilities enable marketing teams to generate high-quality content at scale."
RIGHT: "The platform writes like your best copywriter. At ten times the speed."
```

### 5. Contractions Everywhere
Written English sounds stiff when read aloud. Contractions fix this.

```
WRONG: "You will not believe how much time you are saving."
RIGHT: "You won't believe how much time you're saving."
```

### 6. Active Voice
Passive constructions slow delivery and confuse listeners.

```
WRONG: "Content is generated by the AI based on your brand guidelines."
RIGHT: "The AI generates content using your brand guidelines."
```

### 7. Emotional Anchors
Plant 2-3 emotional moments: frustration, relief, excitement, pride. Listeners remember feelings.

```
[FRUSTRATION ANCHOR]
You've rewritten that headline seven times. It still isn't right.

[RELIEF ANCHOR]
PureBrain suggests three options in seconds. All on-brand. All ready to test.
```

### 8. Reflection Closes
End sections or the full piece with a question or statement that lands in silence.

```
WRONG ending: "...and that's why PureBrain is the right choice for your team."
RIGHT ending: "The question isn't whether AI will transform your marketing. It already has. The question is: are you using it?"
```

### 9. Rhythm Variation
Alternate long and short sentences to create natural spoken cadence.

```
Long: "Most marketing teams spend their days bouncing between tools, copying and pasting, reformatting the same content for different platforms."
Short: "There's a better way."
Long: "PureBrain brings everything into one place..."
Short: "One login. One workflow."
```

### 10. Direct Address ("You")
Speak directly to the listener at least once every 3-4 sentences.

```
WRONG: "Marketing teams often struggle with consistency."
RIGHT: "Your team knows the struggle. The brand voice guide that nobody reads. The off-brand post that went live anyway."
```

## Before / After Example

**BEFORE (Written prose):**
"PureBrain is an AI-powered marketing platform designed to help teams streamline content creation, improve brand consistency, and measure campaign performance more effectively. The platform integrates with existing tools and provides AI-generated content suggestions based on approved brand guidelines."

**AFTER (Audio-optimized):**
"Your brand has a voice. [SHORT PAUSE] Most content doesn't use it.

PureBrain changes that. It's the AI that knows your guidelines, your tone, your customer. [PAUSE]

Every piece of content it suggests? On-brand from the first word.

You don't edit for tone anymore. You edit for preference."

## Quick Checklist

Before delivering any audio-destined script, verify:
- [ ] Hook in first 10 words
- [ ] At least 2 [PAUSE] markers
- [ ] Average sentence length under 15 words
- [ ] All contractions applied
- [ ] No passive voice constructions
- [ ] At least 2 emotional anchors
- [ ] Reflection close at end
- [ ] "You" appears at least 1x per paragraph
